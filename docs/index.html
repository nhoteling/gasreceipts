Analysis of Gas Receipts

Nathan Hoteling

October 2024


This analysis provides a summary of data collected from gas receipts for all vehicles I have owned from April 2005 to October 2024, a span of 19.5 years. Over this time period we drove a total of 331,440 miles and spent $34,622.47 on gas.



Total Miles

The plot below shows the number of miles driven per month for all vehicles over this period. Note that gaps in the line chart represent months with no data; in some cases this was because we didn’t purchase gas during that month and in others it is because gas receipts were lost.

Details: Seasonality


There are some seasonal patterns in the number of miles driven over the course of the year.




Gas Mileage

The plot below shows the monthly gas mileage for each vehicle over this span. Each data point represents the monthly aggregate for a particular vehicle, and the curves are smoothed lines obtained from a time-series decomposition.

Details: Gas Mileage


These histograms compare the monthly gas mileage aggregates to values quoted on https://www.fueleconomy.gov/ for each vehicle.

Details: Seasonality


Seasonal fluctuations in gas mileage were determined from a time series decomposition. The values vary for each vehicle:
  • For the Cavalier the magnitude is 2.9 mpg
  • For the Corolla the magnitude is 6.7 mpg
  • For the RAV4 the magnitude is 6.5 mpg




Gas Prices

The plot below compares what we paid for gas with the national average monthly gas price for regular grade fuel according to the US Energy Information Administration. Each data point represents the monthly aggregate for all gas purchased during a particular month, and the trendline indicates the national average.

Details: Seasonality


The seasonal fluctuations in the gas prices amount to $0.47 , with the peak price in June , and the lowest in January .
